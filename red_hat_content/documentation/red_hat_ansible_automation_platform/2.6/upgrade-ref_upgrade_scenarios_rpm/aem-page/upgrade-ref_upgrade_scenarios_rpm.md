# Supported RPM-based upgrade and migration scenarios

Find the supported upgrade paths for your RPM-based Ansible Automation Platform deployment. This helps you plan the necessary steps for a smooth upgrade.

## RPM-based Ansible Automation Platform 2.4 on RHEL 8

Warning:

Ansible Automation Platform 2.6 is the last version that supports RPM-based deployments. To upgrade to Ansible Automation Platform 2.7, migrate to a containerized or OpenShift Container Platform deployment first.

| ------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| <br>RPM-based Ansible Automation Platform 2.4 on RHEL 8 | <br>RPM-based Ansible Automation Platform 2.6 on RHEL 9             | [Backup your deployment of 2.4 RPM](https://docs.redhat.com/en/documentation/red_hat_ansible_automation_platform/2.4/html/automation_controller_administration_guide/controller-backup-and-restore) on RHEL 8, then restore to a RHEL 9 environment running a fresh installation of RPM 2.4.[Upgrade your RPM deployment](/documentation/en-us/red_hat_ansible_automation_platform/2.6/upgrade-con_aap_upgrade_overview#con-upgrade-improvements-overview "Ansible Automation Platform 2.6 includes changes that improve the overall platform upgrade experience.") from 2.4 to 2.6.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |

| <br>RPM-based Ansible Automation Platform 2.4 on RHEL 8 | <br>Container-based Ansible Automation Platform 2.6 on RHEL 10      | [Backup your deployment of 2.4 RPM](https://docs.redhat.com/en/documentation/red_hat_ansible_automation_platform/2.4/html/automation_controller_administration_guide/controller-backup-and-restore) on RHEL 8, then restore to a RHEL 9 environment running a fresh installation of RPM 2.4.[Upgrade your RPM deployment](/documentation/en-us/red_hat_ansible_automation_platform/2.6/upgrade-con_aap_upgrade_overview#con-upgrade-improvements-overview "Ansible Automation Platform 2.6 includes changes that improve the overall platform upgrade experience.") from 2.4 to 2.6.[Migrate your RPM deployment](/documentation/en-us/red_hat_ansible_automation_platform/2.6/migrate-assembly_migration_prerequisites#rpm-to-containerized-prerequisites "Before migrating from an RPM-based deployment to a container-based deployment, ensure you meet the following prerequisites:") 2.6 to a container deployment 2.6.[Backup your deployment of container 2.6](/documentation/en-us/red_hat_ansible_automation_platform/2.6/administer-back_up_and_restore_your_containerized_deployment#backing-up-containerized-ansible-automation-platform "Perform a backup of your container-based installation of Ansible Automation Platform.") on RHEL 9, then restore to a RHEL 10 environment running a fresh container installation 2.6. |
