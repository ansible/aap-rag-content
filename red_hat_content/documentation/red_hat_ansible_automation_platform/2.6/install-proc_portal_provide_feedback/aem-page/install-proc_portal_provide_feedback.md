# Providing feedback in Ansible automation portal

Ansible automation portal provides a feedback form where you can suggest new features and content, as well as provide general feedback.

## Before you begin

Note:

The feedback form is disabled by default. If the Feedback button is not visible in the console, your administrator must enable it in the portal configuration.

## Procedure

1.  Click Feedback in the Ansible automation portal console to display the feedback form.
2.  Enter the feedback you want to provide.
3.  Select the I understand that feedback is shared with Red Hat checkbox.
4.  Click Submit.

## What to do next

Note:

To ensure that Red Hat receives your feedback, exclude your Ansible automation portal URL in any browser ad blockers or privacy tools.
