# Manage your organization's automation content

Organize, store, distribute, secure, and govern your organization's automation content, including roles, collections, and execution environments.

Ansible Automation Platform’s automation hub serves as the centralized source of truth for your organization's automation assets.

Instead of developers keeping custom modules, roles, and playbooks scattered across disparate repositories or local machines, private automation hub provides a unified registry for managing Ansible Content Collections and execution environments. This eliminates duplicated effort, allowing your developers to seamlessly discover, share, and reuse established automation code.

By establishing a single repository, you significantly streamline the development lifecycle, ensuring your team is always building upon the most current and reliable foundations.

From a management and governance perspective, automation hub empowers you to enforce strict quality control and security standards before content is deployed enterprise-wide. It features robust role-based access control (RBAC) and a built-in content curation process, allowing you to establish workflows where senior developers can review, test, and approve collections before they are published to the broader organization.

Furthermore, it allows you to safely synchronize with Red Hat's public automation hub, blending certified, vendor-supported content with your team's proprietary IP in one secure location. Ultimately, automation hub helps you transform your team's output from fragmented scripts into a trusted, scalable catalog of enterprise-grade automation.
