# 4. About the Job Explorer
## 4.2. Drilling down into cluster data
### 4.2.1. Example: Reviewing failed jobs




You can view more detail about failed jobs across your organization by drilling down on the graph on the **Cluster** view and using the **Job Explorer** to refine results.

Clicking on a specific portion in a graph will open that information in the **Job Explorer** , preserving contextual information created when using filters on the **Clusters** view.

**Procedure**

1. From the navigation panel, selectAutomation Analytics→Clusters.
1. Using the filter lists in the toolbar, you can apply filters for clusters and time range of your choosing.


**Verification**

1. Click a segment on the graph.

You are redirected to the **Job Explorer** view, and presented with a list of successful and failed jobs corresponding to that day on the bar graph.

To view only failed jobs:


1. Select **Status** from the **Filter by** list.
1. Select the **Failed** filter.

The view is updated to show only failed jobs run on that day. Add additional context to the view by applying additional filters and selecting attributes to sort results. Link out and review more information for failed jobs on the automation controller job details page.
