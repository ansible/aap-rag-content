# 2. Configuring authentication in the Ansible Automation Platform
## 2.8. Managing authentication in Ansible Automation Platform
### 2.8.2. Searching for an authenticator




You can search for a previously configured authenticator from the Authentication list view.

**Procedure**

1. From the navigation panel, selectAccess Management→Authentication Methods.
1. In the search bar, enter an appropriate keyword for the authentication method you want to search for and click the arrow icon.
1. If you do not find what you are looking for, you can narrow your search. From the filter list, select **Name** or **Authentication type** depending on the search term you want to use.
1. Scroll through the list of search results and select the authenticator you want to review.
