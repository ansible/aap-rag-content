# Supported container-based upgrade and migration scenarios

Find the supported upgrade paths for your container-based Ansible Automation Platform deployment. This helps you plan the necessary steps for a smooth upgrade.

| <br>Container-based Ansible Automation Platform 2.5 on RHEL 9 | <br>Container-based Ansible Automation Platform 2.6 on RHEL 10      | [Backup your container deployment of 2.5](https://docs.redhat.com/en/documentation/red_hat_ansible_automation_platform/2.5/html-single/containerized_installation/index#backing-up-containerized-ansible-automation-platform) on a RHEL 9 environment, then restore to a RHEL 10 environment running a fresh container installation 2.5.[Upgrade your container deployment](/documentation/en-us/red_hat_ansible_automation_platform/2.6/upgrade-proc_update_aap_container "Perform an upgrade of containerized Ansible Automation Platform.") from 2.5 to 2.6.                                                                    |
| <br>Container-based Ansible Automation Platform 2.5 on RHEL 9 | <br>Ansible Automation Platform on OpenShift Container Platform 2.6 | [Upgrade your container deployment](/documentation/en-us/red_hat_ansible_automation_platform/2.6/upgrade-proc_update_aap_container "Perform an upgrade of containerized Ansible Automation Platform.") from 2.5 to 2.6.[Migrate your container deployment](/documentation/en-us/red_hat_ansible_automation_platform/2.6/migrate-assembly_migration_prerequisites#containerized-to-ocp-prerequisites "Before migrating from a container-based deployment to an OpenShift Container Platform deployment, ensure that you meet the following prerequisites:") 2.6 to Ansible Automation Platform on OpenShift Container Platform 2.6. |
