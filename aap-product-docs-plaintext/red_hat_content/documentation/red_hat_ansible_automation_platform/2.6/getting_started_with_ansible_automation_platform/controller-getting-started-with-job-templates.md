# 3. Getting started as an automation developer
## 3.13. Work with job templates
### 3.13.1. Getting started with job templates




Job templates are definitions and sets of parameters for running an Ansible job. A job template ties together all the necessary information to launch an Ansible job.

As part of the initial setup, a **Demo Job Template** is created for you.

**Procedure**

1. To review existing templates, selectAutomation Execution→Templatesfrom the navigation panel.
1. ClickDemo Job Templateto view its details.
