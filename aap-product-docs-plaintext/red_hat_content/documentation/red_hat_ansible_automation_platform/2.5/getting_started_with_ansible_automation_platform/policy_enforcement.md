# 1. Key functionality and concepts
## 1.2. Automation execution
### 1.2.2. Policy enforcement




Policy enforcement at automation runtime is a feature that uses encoded rules to define, manage, and enforce policies that govern how your users interact with your Ansible Automation Platform instance. Policy enforcement automates policy management, improving security, compliance, and efficiency. Policy enforcement points can be configured at the level of the inventory, job template, or organization. For more, see [Implementing policy enforcement](https://docs.redhat.com/en/documentation/red_hat_ansible_automation_platform/2.5/html/configuring_automation_execution/controller-pac) in the Configuring automation execution guide.
