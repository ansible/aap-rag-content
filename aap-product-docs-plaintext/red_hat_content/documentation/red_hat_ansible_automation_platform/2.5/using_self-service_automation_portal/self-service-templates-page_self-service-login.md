# 2. Logging in to self-service automation portal
## 2.2. Viewing templates




**Prerequisites**

1. You have signed in to self-service automation portal.


**Procedure**

1. In a browser, sign in to self-service automation portal.
1. In the navigation pane, select **Templates** to open a landing page where tiles are displayed, representing the templates that you have access to.
