# 5. Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform Service on AWS Service Definition
## 5.4. Support




Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform Service on AWS includes Red Hat Premium Support, accessible through the [Red Hat Customer Portal](https://access.redhat.com/support) .

For support response times, refer to the [Production Support Terms of Service](https://access.redhat.com/support/offerings/production/sla) .

AWS support is subject to the customer’s existing support contract with AWS.
